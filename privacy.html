Privacy Policy

This is the Privacy Policy for www.ladies-market.hk and describes what information is collected by the site, and by its partners, and how it is used.

If you require any more information or have any questions about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us by email.

At www.ladies-market.hk, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy document outlines the types of personal information is received and collected by www.ladies-market.hk and how it is used.

Log Files
Like many other Web sites, www.ladies-market.hk makes use of log files. The information inside the log files includes internet protocol ( IP ) addresses, type of browser, Internet Service Provider ( ISP ), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks to analyze trends, administer the site, track users movement around the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses, and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable.

Cookies and Web Beacons
www.ladies-market.hk does use cookies to store information about visitors preferences, record user-specific information on which pages the user access or visit, customize Web page content based on visitors browser type or other information that the visitor sends via their browser.

Google, Google Analytics and DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on www.ladies-market.hk, to record information about website usage including how visitors reach the website, what pages they load, and when or if they return.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to your users based on their visit to www.ladies-market.hk and other sites on the Internet.

www.ladies-market.hk does now or may in the future any of these Google facilities   

  • Remarketing with Google Analytics
  • Google Display Network Impression Reporting
  • Google Analytics Demographics and Interest Reporting
  • Integrated services that require Google Analytics to collect data via advertising cookies and anonymous identifiers
  • Affinity audiences
  • Custom affinity audiences
  • In-market audiences
  • Similar audiences
  • Demographic and location targeting
  • Demographics and Interest Reports

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at the following URL - https://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html

Some of our partners may use cookies and web beacons on our site. Our partners include .......

  • Google Adsense, Google Analytics, DoubleClick
  • Mailchimp
  • Addthis
  • Quantcast

These third-party ad servers or ad networks use technology to the advertisements and links that appear on www.ladies-market.hk send directly to your browsers. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. Other technologies ( such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons ) may also be used by the third-party ad networks to measure the effectiveness of their advertisements and / or to personalize the advertising content that you see.

www.ladies-market.hk has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

You should consult the respective privacy policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information on their practices as well as for instructions about how to opt-out of certain practices. www.ladies-market.hk's privacy policy does not apply to, and we cannot control the activities of, such other advertisers or web sites.

If you wish to disable cookies, you may do so through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found at the browsers' respective websites.

Personally Identifiable Information

www.ladies-market.hk does not seek to collect Personally Identifiable Information about any visitor unless they explicitly provide the information, such as by signing up for a mailing list or using a contact form.

At no point will personally identifiable information be combined with non-personally identifiable information in an attempt to remove anonymity.

Information collected via mailing list sign up forms or contact forms or emails will only be used for the purposes related to that mailing list or contact or email.  It will not be passed to third parties except when technically necessary when third parties are performing actions on behalf of www.ladies-market.hk, e.g. in the case of using Mailchimp to send newsletters on our behalf.

Use of Information

Information collected on this site anonymously, such as by server web logs or analytics packages, including third party analytics packages, will be used to better understand our visitors so that we can provide the best possible relevant content, including editorial, relevant advertising and others.
