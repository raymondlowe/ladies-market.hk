Soft toys at Ladies market

As well as Hong Kong Logo goods there are many stalls in the Lady Market which sell items not only for Hong Kong ladies but also specifically for tourists and visitors to the region.  It is very common for visitors to want to purchase souvenirs to take back as presents to friends and family, perhaps grandchildren or nieces may appreciate some authentic Chinese garments.  Or some soft toys of famous cartoon characters, all of which can be purchased in ladies' market for very reasonable prices.

SHOPPING AND FOOD

While often visited for the view the visitor should not forget that The Market encompasses a wide range of shopping and eating choices. Mongkok is well known for snack food and as well as budget choices.
