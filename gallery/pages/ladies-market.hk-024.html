Dresses at Ladies Market

Despite being a street market the Ladies Market is not all about casual things.  It is above all a lady market Hong Kong and as such don't be surprised to find special little luxury items such as these fancy dresses which here in Hong Kong would probably be worn to a wedding.

Ladies in other countries might find them suitable for a fancy party, a Prom Ball or a teen birthday party in some parties for the world.

You will find in ladies market Hongkong a wide range of items which while not entirely unique are unusual, and certainly available at very modest prices.

SHOPPING AND FOOD

While often visited for the view the visitor should not forget that The Market encompasses a wide range of shopping and eating choices. Mongkok is well known for snack food and as well as budget choices.
